The executive board manages the day-to-day affairs of CSGO and also regularly meets with the department chair to discuss grad student concerns. The treasurer can help provide funding provide funding for meetings.

Join the CSGO Mailing List to receive reminders and minutes about meetings and get notified of any changes, amendments and other activities. All computer science graduate students are welcome to be involved in CSGO activities. For Fall 2018, the meeting time and location is still being determined.

If you would like to get in touch with the CSGO anonymously, we have an electronic suggestion box as well as a mailbox in the Ph.D. student mailroom (Gates Hall room 120).
