The executive board manages the day-to-day affairs of CSGO and also regularly meets with the department chair to discuss grad student concerns. The treasurer can help provide funding provide funding for meetings.

Join the CSGO Mailing List to receive reminders and minutes about meetings and get notified of any changes, amendments and other activities. All computer science graduate students are welcome to be involved in CSGO activities. Meetings take place every other Thursday at 12:00pm in 406 Gates Hall.
